Charlie Charlie Challenge - A Multiplayer Co-op Horror Game

Charlie Charlie Challenge is a game I made during the winter semester of my second year, its a multiplayer co-op horror game based off the childs game. This is my best game ive made and the most polish has gone into this one. After release I later found one twitch streamer played it for three hours straight until he beat it and I was very happy with this result.

I highly suggest you give this one a try, especially if you are looking at my portfolio for a game dev position. This shows my best work and you can contact me and ask for a free key. The whole thing was done solo and many of the models were also made by myself and 100% of the code was written by me.

  • Fully replicated multiplayer inventory system implemented using C++. Implements drop items and different interactions with multiple items. Applies standard OOP concepts like abstraction and inheritance for a clean and concise codebase.
  • Full downed system and revive system.
  • Server browser and a full settings menu.
  • Dynamic AI that adapts to any number of players gets harder and harder as game goes on.
  • Advanced replicated items, such as a board that detects specific voip phrases, a EMF reader.
  • Full in game guide and pre lobby guide.
  • Dyanimc gameplay, game is different everytime you play which is why this is by far the best game ive done as its actually fun for me as well. I dont really know where things will spawn or what he will be doing.
  • Emphasis on UI and slightly guiding player
  • Advanced post FX including fisheye, DOF, physics based vignette
  • Heavy attention to sound and game design.
  • Steam achievements and keeping track of in game stats so player knows how game went.

This is my best game dev project and took a good amount of time. Everything if fully replicated and I spent a good amount of time on polishing / bug fixing. I highly suggest you try it yourself or even try it with me to see my full skills. Full gameplay and trailers can be found on my youtube.
